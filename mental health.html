A Step Towards Healthier Mental Stability


Our names are April Bolton and Brishay Francis and we'd like to focus on bettering the mental health amongst our youth of today. Rates of youth with severe depression increased from 5.9% in 2012 to 8.2% in 2015, and we believe they continue to increase over time. We've noticed most of our peers a displaying signs of mental illness, and most of us cope amongst ourselves instead of seeking help elsewhere, or use the internet to find ways to make due. We figured since everyone is on their phones right about now, why not make an app to help our youth better their mental health. So we're working on an app that includes self help tasks, on-call therapist to talk to through the app, a "happy game" known as Happy Fluorescence and much more.
